Overview
        
The Red Army was a Scratch nation founded on November 1st, 2024, the day the [Classified] Union fell under Popcorn Control. It had 386 followers at its peak.
 

        
History

After Ronaldo tricked MasterX into giving him host of the [Classified] Union, MasterX quickly began to rebuild, creating the Red Army that same day.
They originally used the flag below:

Although (unfortunately) the image quality is poor, one can see the [Classified] flag in the canton (upper left corner). 
This was an attempt to showcase the Red Army's origins. 
On the same day that the Red Army was founded, "The Red War" began with the Popcorn Uprising.

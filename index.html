Overview
        
The Red Army was a Scratch nation founded on November 1st, 2024, the day the [Classified] Union fell under Popcorn Control. It had 386 followers at its peak.
 

        
History

After Ronaldo tricked MasterX into giving him host of the [Classified] Union, MasterX quickly began to rebuild, creating the Red Army that same day.
